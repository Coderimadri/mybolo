Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978.


The St. Thomas School Dhurwa is home to a beautiful and spiritually significant school church. This sacred space provides a serene sanctuary for students and faculty to gather for worship, reflection, and spiritual guidance. With its serene atmosphere and rich religious heritage, the school church plays a vital role in nurturing the faith and values of the school community.

School Library

Was established in 1980.


The school library is a wonderful place, espically for book afflindos. It is full of fantasting books, including fairy tales, novels, biographies, etc. There is a library in primary as well as the secondary side. the libraries of both are excellent, along with books suitable for readers. In the secondary side, the children are even allowed to borrow the books and to return it after reading. It is a truely amazing place and favourite of many young book afflindos.

School Auditorium

Was established in 1982.


The St. Thomas School Dhurwa boasts a remarkable auditorium, a haven for creativity and culture within its academic campus. This state-of-the-art facility serves as a hub for various school events, including theatrical performances, academic seminars, and cultural celebrations. With its modern amenities and welcoming ambiance, the school auditorium stands as a vital center for fostering talent and fostering a sense of community among students and staff.
